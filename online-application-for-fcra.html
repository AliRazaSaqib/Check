FCRA online facility for grant of prior approval was introduced by Ministry of Home Affairs on experimental basis w.e.f. 12.11.2007. Public in general and the professionals and NGOs, in particular are hereby notified that this facility has actually been made mandatory w.e.f. 01.08.2008 to facilitate associations to file their applications for previous approval to accept foreign contribution under the Foreign Contribution Regulation Act 1976, online on the Ministry of Home Affairs website: http://www.mha.nic.in. This center of online status enquiry is likewise readily available through this site. The above facility aims to offer an efficient, transparent and liable delivery system, for prompt and responsive service to stakeholders. Further, it will decrease postal and interaction delays and will assist in online interactions and deals. This will also considerably reduce paper work and automate work-flow. Ministry of Home Affairs has actually begun accepting online filing of complex FC-3 forms. Because the papers formats are still to be sent out to Ministry, the system is not of much help. The system, expects the data to be filled online, as opposed to the Income Tax, where form is prepared on a computer and then uploaded to the system.
